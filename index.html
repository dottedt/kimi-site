About Me

Kimi \k(i)-mi\

Kimi is a girl's name of Japanese origin meaning “righteous, beautiful story, she who is without equal and royal”.

Its Swedish origin also means “upright”.

The English meaning of Kimi is "Cyneburg's field” or “royal fortress".

It is the Hawaiian pet form of the name “Kimberly”.

And the Native American Algonquin translation is "secret."

It is likewise a Finnish boy’s name standing for “noble” and is held by the famous Finnish race-car driver Kimi Räikkönen.

According to some sources, people with this name are “extremely reliable and have a strong inner desire to serve humanity and give to others by sharing knowledge, experience and creative and artistic ability.”

And just as the name “Kimi” has several different meanings, there are several different facets that make up my persona. Which lends itself beautifully to generating creative that relates to the limitless number of audiences I write for.
